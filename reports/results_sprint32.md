### Methods
A very interesting method has been found: the "Invitation rule" as proposed by the [Data Coalitie](https://datagedrevenonderzoekmbo.nl/themas/voorspelmodel/praktijkpilot-de-uitnodigingsregel/). It provides a solid method description of a practical implementation of a ML-driven student drop-out prediction model.
